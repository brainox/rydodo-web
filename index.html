Privacy Policy for Rydodo

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Rydodo ("we", "us", or "the app") helps members of running and hiking clubs coordinate carpools to club events. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Rydodo you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information to operate the app:

DataWhy we collect it
Name To identify you to other members of your club and on rides you offer or join.
Email address Provided through Google or Apple sign-in. Used to create and secure your account.
Phone number Used to connect drivers and riders after a ride is confirmed (for example, via a WhatsApp link). Your phone number stays hidden from other members until you and the other party are matched and confirmed.
Location information Pickup points, event locations, and meeting spots you enter or select so carpools can be coordinated. We do not continuously track your device in the background.
Push notification tokens A device identifier used to deliver notifications about ride requests, confirmations, and club activity.
Usage & analytics data Information about how you use the app (screens viewed, actions taken, device and app version) to understand usage and improve the product.
Crash & diagnostic data Crash logs and performance data to find and fix bugs and keep the app stable.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and club memberships.
  • To let you offer rides, request seats, and coordinate carpools with other members.
  • To reveal contact details (phone number) only to a matched and confirmed driver/rider.
  • To send push notifications related to your rides and clubs.
  • To analyze usage and diagnose crashes so we can improve and maintain the app.
  • To protect against fraud, abuse, and violations of our terms.

3. How Information Is Shared

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as follows:

  • With other members — your name and, after a confirmed match, your phone number are shared with the driver or rider you are matched with.
  • With service providers — we use trusted third parties to operate the app, including Google Firebase (authentication, database, cloud messaging, analytics, and crash reporting). These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy terms.
  • For legal reasons — if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users or the public.

Google's handling of data through Firebase is described in the Firebase Privacy and Security documentation and the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

We keep your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we must retain it to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data.
  • You can delete your account, which removes your profile and associated personal data.
  • You can disable push notifications in your device settings at any time.
  • Depending on your location, you may have rights under the GDPR (EU/UK) or CCPA (California), including the right to access, port, or erase your data and to object to certain processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

Rydodo is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your data and limit access to it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app. Continued use of Rydodo after changes means you accept the updated policy.
