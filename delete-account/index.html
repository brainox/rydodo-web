Delete Your Rydodo Account

Last updated: 30 June 2026

This page explains how to delete your Rydodo account and the personal data associated with it. Rydodo helps members of running and hiking clubs coordinate carpools.

You can delete your account in two ways: directly inside the app, or by email.

Option 1 — Delete in the app

The fastest way is to delete your account from within Rydodo:

  1. Open the Rydodo app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Under Account, tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm when prompted.

Your account and associated data are then deleted automatically. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email us from the address linked to your account:

macmartins081@gmail.com

Use the subject line "Delete my Rydodo account" and include the email address you signed up with (via Google or Apple). We will verify the request and process it.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, we remove the personal data tied to it, including:

  • Your name and profile information
  • Your email address and account identifiers
  • Your phone number
  • Pickup/location points you entered
  • Your push notification token
  • Your ride and club activity associated with your account

What may be retained

We may retain limited information where required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or prevent fraud and abuse. Any retained data is kept only for as long as necessary and is then deleted.

Timeframe

We process deletion requests within 30 days of verifying your identity. You will receive a confirmation once your account and data have been deleted.

Questions

For questions about account deletion or your data, contact macmartins081@gmail.com.
